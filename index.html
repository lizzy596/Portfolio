About

Hi! I'm a self-taught web-developer with a focus on front-end developement. I've taken formal courses in HTML,CSS, Javascript, Responsive Design, Adobe Photoshop, UX/UI and E-commerce via Austin CC's Web Developer Program. I've also been working steadily through the online bootcamp The Odin Project through which I have learned: Git, Github, Linting, Test-Driven Development using Jest, ES6 Modules, WebPack, API Calls and React. I am very comfortable building apps in HTML/CSS, Vanilla JS and React.

In another life, I was in the medical field and have a B.S. in Economics. I have enjoyed making this career change as I find the field of web-development rewarding, creative, collaborative and with seemingly endless new things to learn. I look forward to working with you on your next project!
